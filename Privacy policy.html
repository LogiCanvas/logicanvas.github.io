Privacy Policy

LogiCanvas ("the Agency", "We", "Us", "Our") operates as an independent digital consultancy brand specializing in AI Automation, Logo Designing, and Landing Page Designing. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

1. Data Collection & Consent

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics engines to collect sensitive personal profile data. We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our contact forms, booking links, or reach out directly via email. This information includes:

  • Your Name and Personal/Official Email Address.
  • Your Company Name and Website URL.
  • Project Descriptions, Technical Workflows, and Design Briefs.

2. Purpose and Use of Data Processing

In strict compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 of India, your data is processed only for the following legitimate business requirements:

  • To respond to your inquiries and compile custom technical proposals.
  • To execute and deliver contracted AI automation and web layout designs.
  • To communicate project milestones, deliverables, and financial invoices.

We maintain a strict zero-spam policy. Your shared corporate or personal data will never be sold, leased, or shared with third-party marketing companies.

3. Data Retention, Security, and User Rights

Security: We deploy standardized access controls and encryption frameworks to secure your data storage channels.

Retention: Your data is retained strictly as long as necessary to complete your project scope and fulfill individual tax filing obligations under Indian law.

Your Rights: Under the DPDP Act, 2023, you retain absolute rights to review, update, or request the erasure of your personal records from our database by emailing us directly.
